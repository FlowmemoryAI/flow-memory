# Code of Conduct

Flow Memory uses the Contributor Covenant as its behavioral baseline for public collaboration.

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and assume good intent;
- keep technical criticism specific, evidence-backed, and actionable;
- avoid harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, or discriminatory language;
- respect security-sensitive information and responsible disclosure boundaries;
- prioritize user safety, privacy, and reliability when discussing agent, wallet, sandbox, or automation features.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, community spaces, and maintainer interactions related to Flow Memory.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior is unsafe, abusive, or disruptive. Security-sensitive incidents should be reported through the process in `SECURITY.md` rather than posted publicly.

## Attribution

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for Flow Memory's safety-critical agent infrastructure context.
